Who We Are

Established in 2004, MCC Limpopo is a technology-driven company specializing in construction, tiling, and mining. With over two decades of experience, we have been a trusted manufacturer and supplier to South Africa's major growth industries, offering innovative products, deep technical expertise, and exceptional service.

Our journey began with a commitment to quality and innovation. Originally based at 43 Staal Street in Ladine since 2006, our continued growth prompted a move to a larger facility at 24, 20th Street, Industria, Polokwane — allowing us to better serve our growing client base and expand our production capabilities.

MCC Limpopo was instrumental in the development of the CTM tile adhesive brand until 2013 and has been a reliable supplier to independent hardware stores across Limpopo since 2006. We are proud to be the only manufacturer in the industry currently offering a lightweight tile adhesive — setting us apart through innovation and responsiveness to market needs.

Our mission is to deliver new and forward-thinking solutions to both local and international construction markets. With a focus on excellence, we aim to lead the industry in the manufacture and supply of cementitious and resin-based products. Through superior service and high-quality offerings, MCC Limpopo continues to build lasting value in the construction and allied industries.
